Privacy Policy

How we protect your information.

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

At AskMyGrandpa, we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to connect our community.

2. Information We Collect

When you register as a Grandpa or submit a question, we collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Profile Details: Photos, skill sets, and personal notes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to facilitate connections between Users and Grandpas. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

4. Public Visibility

If you register as a Grandpa:

  • Your First Name (e.g., "Grandpa Joe") will be visible to the public.
  • Your Photos and Skills will be visible to the public.
  • Your Specific Address, Phone Number, and Email will NOT be publicly displayed on the website. These are only shared privately when a connection is made and you consent to it.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@askmygrandpa.com.
